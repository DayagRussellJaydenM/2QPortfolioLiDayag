DESCRIPTION:

Trial Chambers is a famous structure in Minecraft that can be considered as one of the most difficult structures to beat/get loot from. It was added in update 1.21 in where it is a naturally generated underground structure (around y= -40 to -20 ) and contains various mobs from trial and ominous trial spawners.

How to Locate:

You can locate this structure by using an explorer map of the trial chameber which can be bought from a cartographer villager when it reaches level 3 or you can use /locate structure command.

Mobs:

Loot:

Trial Chambers carry a variety of loot and access to potions and custom arrows for low level players. To get keys to unlock vaults, you have to beat the multiple mobs that will spawn from the multiple spawners around the structure. The list of loot from vaults (not including chests and ominous trial vaults) and their chances are in this Woodland Mansion
