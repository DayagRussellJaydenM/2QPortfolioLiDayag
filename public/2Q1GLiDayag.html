Trial Chamber

Image of the inside of a Trial Chamber

DESCRIPTION:

Trial Chambers is a famous structure in Minecraft that can be considered as one of the most difficult structures to beat/get loot from. It provides good loot such as the Heavy Core that can be used to craft one of the most overpowered weapons in the game, the Mace. Other loot include enchanted diamond/iron gear, golden apple, diamonds, etc.
