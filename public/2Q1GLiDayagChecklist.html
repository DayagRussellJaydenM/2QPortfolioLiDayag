Preparation Checklist for Trial Chamber

At least an iron sword/axe (preferrably enchanted)
A shield, and at least full iron armor (preferrably enchanted)
Food that replenishes a lot of hunger such as cooked steak (at least half a stack)
At least 16 wood logs for crafting
A few stacks of easily breakable building blocks
A few bundles to store more loot
*Optional but highly recommended: A bow and at least a stack of arrows
*Optional but recommended: A few Golden Apples for healing
*Optional but recommended: A few potions such as strength to help with fighting mobs
*Optional: An Ominous Bottle (only if you want to start an ominous trial). You can be lucky and get it from the regular trials
*Optional: A Totem of Undying for dangerous situations in where you might die
